About GramsToOunces.net

Your trusted source for accurate weight conversions since 2024

Our Mission

GramsToOunces.net was created with a simple mission: to provide free, accurate, and easy-to-use weight conversion tools for everyone. Whether you're a home cook following international recipes, a student working on science assignments, or a professional needing quick unit conversions, we're here to help.

We believe that useful tools should be accessible to all, without ads, paywalls, or unnecessary complexity. Just enter your values and get instant, precise results.

Why Trust Us

Our conversion tools are built on internationally recognized standards and verified data sources:

  • NIST Standards: All conversion factors follow guidelines from the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST Handbook 44)
  • International Standards: Based on the International System of Units (SI) as defined by the International Bureau of Weights and Measures (BIPM)
  • ISO Compliance: Adheres to ISO 80000-3:2006 standards for quantities and units
  • Precision: Calculations accurate to at least 6 decimal places, displayed at 3 decimal places for practical use
  • Regular Verification: All conversion factors are periodically reviewed and updated

What We Offer

Our growing collection of conversion tools includes:

  • Grams to Ounces (and reverse)
  • Grams to Pounds, Kilograms, Stones
  • Grams to Grains, Drams, Tolas
  • Grams to Quarters, Hundredweights, Tons
  • All conversions work bidirectionally in real-time
