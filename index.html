About Weight Units

The gram (g) is a metric unit of weight defined as one thousandth of a kilogram. It's widely used in scientific contexts and in most countries worldwide.

The ounce (oz) is a unit of weight used primarily in the United States, United Kingdom, and other countries using the imperial system. One ounce equals approximately 28.35 grams.

Practical Applications

Converting between grams and ounces is essential for:

  • Measuring ingredients accurately
  • Scientific experiments and measurements
  • Calculating postage for international shipping
  • Determining medication dosages

Frequently Asked Questions

How many grams are in an ounce?

One ounce equals approximately 28.35 grams. More precisely, 1 oz = 28.34952 grams. This is the standard conversion used for most weight measurements.

How do I convert 100 grams to ounces?

To convert 100 grams to ounces, multiply by 0.03527396. So 100g = 3.527 oz. You can use our calculator above for instant results.

Is this converter accurate for cooking?

Yes! Our converter uses the official conversion factor (1 oz = 28.34952g) and is accurate to 3 decimal places, making it perfect for cooking, baking, and precise recipe measurements.

What's the difference between ounces and fluid ounces?

Ounces (oz) measure weight/mass, while fluid ounces (fl oz) measure volume. This converter handles weight ounces only. For liquids, density matters in weight-to-volume conversions.

Can I use this for gold or precious metals?

Yes, but note that precious metals are often measured in troy ounces (31.1g), not regular ounces (28.35g). For gold calculations, consider using a dedicated precious metals converter.
