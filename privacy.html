Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At GramsToOunces.net, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our weight conversion tools.

The short version: We collect minimal anonymous data to improve our service. We never sell your data. We don't require accounts or personal information to use our tools.

Information We Collect

Anonymous Usage Data

We use Google Analytics (ID: G-PDSC317HG8) to understand how visitors use our website. This service automatically collects:

  • Pages you visit on our site
  • Time spent on each page
  • Browser type and version (e.g., Chrome 120, Safari 17)
  • Device type (Desktop, Mobile, Tablet)
  • Operating system (e.g., Windows, macOS, iOS, Android)
  • General geographic location (country and city level only)
  • Referring website (if you came from a search engine or another site)
  • Screen resolution

Note: All of this data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Google Analytics cookies: To track anonymous usage statistics
  • No tracking cookies: We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies

What We DON'T Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what we don't collect:

  • ❌ Personal identification information (name, address, etc.)
  • ❌ Email addresses (unless you contact us directly)
  • ❌ Phone numbers
  • ❌ Payment information (our service is completely free)
  • ❌ Login credentials (we don't have user accounts)
  • ❌ The actual values you enter in our converters
  • ❌ Your precise GPS location
  • ❌ Any data from your camera or microphone

How We Use Information

The anonymous data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand which conversion tools are most popular
  • Identify technical issues and bugs
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Make decisions about new features to add
  • Ensure our site works well on different devices and browsers

We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google uses the collected data to track and monitor the use of our service.

For more information on Google's privacy practices, visit: Google Privacy & Terms

Learn more: Google Fonts Privacy Policy

Your Rights and Choices

Opt-Out of Analytics

You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by:

  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings
  • Using browser extensions that block analytics scripts

Cookie Management

Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. However, please note that blocking cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

Data Access and Deletion

Since we don't collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data for us to provide or delete. The anonymous analytics data we collect cannot be traced back to you.

Children's Privacy

Our service is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since our tools don't require registration or personal information, children can use them safely.

International Users

GramsToOunces.net is operated in the United States. If you are visiting from the European Union or other regions with data protection laws, please note:

  • We only collect anonymous, aggregated data
  • No personal data is transferred or stored
  • You have the same privacy protections regardless of your location

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the limited data we collect:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

However, no internet transmission is completely secure. While we strive to protect data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify users of significant changes via a notice on our homepage
  • Archive previous versions for transparency

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
